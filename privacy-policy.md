# Privacy Policy for Ping Ledger

**Effective Date:** April 26, 2026

## 1. Overview
Ping Ledger is a personal finance tracking app that helps users monitor their transactions by reading and processing SMS messages related to financial activity. Your privacy is important, and this app is designed to handle your data responsibly.

---

## 2. Information We Collect
Ping Ledger may access and process the following data:

- SMS messages related to financial transactions (e.g., bank alerts, payment confirmations)
- Parsed transaction details such as:
  - Amount
  - Transaction type (credit/debit)
  - Merchant or sender name
  - Date and time

We **do not collect personally identifiable information** such as:
- Name
- Email address
- Contacts

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information
The data is used solely to:

- Identify and extract financial transactions from SMS messages
- Display transaction history inside the app
- Provide summaries and insights (daily/monthly spending)

---

## 4. Data Storage
- All data is stored **locally on your device**
- SMS messages are processed on-device and not stored externally
- We do **not upload, share, or sell your data**

---

## 5. Permissions
Ping Ledger requires the following permission:

- **SMS Read Permission**  
  Used to read and process transaction-related messages for expense tracking.

This permission is used strictly for the app’s core functionality and nothing else.

---

## 6. Data Sharing
We **do not share your data** with:
- Third-party services
- Advertisers
- Analytics platforms

---

## 7. Data Security
Your data remains on your device. We do not transmit or store your data on external servers.

---

## 8. User Control
You can:

- Stop using the app at any time
- Revoke SMS permissions from device settings
- Clear app data to remove all stored information

---

## 9. Children's Privacy
Ping Ledger is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

---

This privacy policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
